Timoteo Dinelli

/tiˈmɔteo diˈnɛlli/

"Sempre forte, con coraggio e senza paura".

I am a third year Ph.D. student at Politecnico di Milano, in the Department of Chemistry, Materials and Chemical Engineering Giulio Natta, within the CRECK modeling laboratory. My research activity focuses on the application of data-driven methods to promote the development of chemical kinetics models for the prediction of combustion and pyrolysis of complex fuels. In addition, recently, I started digging my interests into the development of reliable, fast and efficient optimization and numerical methods for the solution of ODE systems of equations. I admit that sometimes I like to write code, mainly C/C++ and Fortran. As you may see my interests are terribly broad, I admit my exuberance, and currently I am not doing anything to constraint it. I am lucky I can pursue interesting and diverse questions, yet still manage to hold down a paying job.

If you are interested in my work do not esitate to contact me. [ Email me]
